Spreadsheet Row VS. Column Multi-Units

In spreadsheets, the values in a row or a column often share the same unit.
We say a spreadsheet has row multi-units if the spreadsheet contains at least two rows with different units.
Similarly, we say a spreadsheet has column multi-units if the spreadsheet contains at least two columns with different units.
