I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am a member of the Michigan Database Group, working with Professor Michael Cafarella. My research focuses on data mining, visualizations and big data.

I have worked on the research project Senbazuru (thousand origami cranes). Senbazuru is a prototype spreadsheet database management system (SSDBMS), which is able to extract structured information from a large number of spreadsheets. It opens up opportunities for integration among spreadsheets and with relational sources.
